Welcome

We sail several classes of dinghies, from the gentle (Mirror, Topper) to the powerful (RS 200, RS300, Laser, Solo).

Just ask for details.

We sail every Sunday, with Thursday evenings during the summer and twice monthly on Saturdays.

Rowing and fishing also take place at Damflask Reservoir.

Currently, no other water-sports are undertaken here. Please see Yorkshire Water for more local information.
